## Kick-Off Meeting

On February 16, 2026, all partners of the EDISON project gathered in Magdeburg for the official project kick-off meeting. Representatives from Otto-von-Guericke-Universität Magdeburg, MartinMechanic GmbH, LUCAS Instruments GmbH, HEMA Formenbau + Kunststoffverarbeitung GmbH, and ZBS e.V. came together to align on project goals, timelines, and work packages.

The meeting marked the formal start of collaboration and set the foundation for the three-year research program aimed at developing context-based photonic intelligence for more efficient and flexible production processes.
